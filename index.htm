Beginner Chess Ideas

This 7 hour course is for absolute beginners to those who know a bit of chess will get to learn the basic ideas of a chess game.
Stuff like how the board is set, how the pieces move, how algebraic notation works are a matter of an hour.
Post that, you'll learn the basic ideas which you need to know to decide moves in chess.

Intermediate Chess Ideas

This 10 hour course is for those who know basics of chess and are willing to learn intermediate tactics for winning more games.
Various patterns in chess, various terms that help you recognize tactics faster, types of positions, types of checkmates and much more.

Opening Repertoire- White

This 7 hour course will be for making an opening repertoire as white for you. Knowing openings always gives you an edge over your opponents, and the more you practice an opening, the better you learn chess principles in general.

Opening Repertoire- Black

This 5 hour course will be for making an opening repertoire as black for you. Knowing how to counter white will help you get better positions in the opening, sometimes you'll be already winning in the opening if you know how to take advantage of opening inaccuracies by white.
